What if you never fully recovered after COVID-19? Young and healthy Australians with the mildest of cases have seen their lives upended by long COVID. We still know so little about the disease, there’s no telling who it will strike and for how long.

For six months after getting COVID-19, Freya Sawbridge was grief stricken for her old body and her old life.

Lots of people fully recover from their COVID-19 infection within 14 days but Freya didn't.

She was stuck on a vicious merry-go-round of symptoms — fever, sore throat, dizziness, muscle spasms, numbness, chest pains and fatigue. The symptoms kept coming around and around and around again.

When long COVID took her health, Freya had nowhere to turn for answers because the reality was, and remains, that we don’t have them.

“Heaps of people say, ‘Oh, I get that and I'm young’, but it just feels different… you'd be mid sentence and then completely forget what you were talking about”
